Demo 'The kidney'

The functional unit of the kidney is the glomerulus where the blood is filtered, resulting in preurine. The glomeruli are located in the cortex. The preurine travels a long way through tubules in the medulla.

De distinction between medulla and cortex can be seen even more clear in this embryonal slide.

Finally, the urine flows via collecting tubes to the papilla that can be seen bulging out into a calyx. Here's an overview of these structures.

The urine leaves the kidney, and passes through the ureter to be collected in the bladder.
